About DELUXE BIO DIGESTER

Greetings from Extreme Engineering Inc!

We install bio digesters - a new sewer system which uses anaerobic digestion, a process where microorganisms break down organic waste in an oxygen-free environment. This process converts the waste into two valuable byproducts:

  • Biogas - A renewable energy source for cooking and lighting
  • Liquid Fertilizer - Nutrient-rich fertilizer for agricultural use

Our bio digesters are suitable for both domestic and commercial applications. We provide complete installation services with all materials included in our pricing.
